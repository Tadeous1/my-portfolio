About Me

Hello! I’m Elisha Oguntade, the mind behind ElishaInsights. As a dedicated data analyst and environmental data enthusiast based in Ogbomoso, Nigeria, I am passionate about transforming numbers into actionable stories that inspire positive change.

With over 1 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in leveraging tools like SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Tableau to help organizations unlock the true value of their data. My journey began with a fascination for numbers and quickly evolved into a career focused on using data to solve real-world challenges, especially those aligned with sustainability and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

My expertise spans across:

  • Data Analytics & Visualization: Building interactive dashboards, reports, and data models that make insights accessible to all stakeholders.
  • Environmental & Academic Data: Analyzing and interpreting complex datasets for sustainability research, policy, and education improvement.
  • Automation & Digital Transformation: Streamlining workflows with Python, VBA, and advanced Excel techniques to boost productivity and data quality.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Bridging the gap between technical data and actionable business decisions through effective communication and storytelling.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring the web, reading, and exploring new technologies that drive digital literacy and community empowerment. I believe that data, when used ethically and creatively, can be a powerful force for progress.
